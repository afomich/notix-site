Reporting a problem

To help us fix things faster, please include:

  • Your device model and iOS version
  • The app version (Settings → About)
  • What you did, what you expected, and what happened instead
  • A screenshot or screen recording, if possible

Subscriptions & billing

Notix Pro subscriptions are billed through your Apple ID. You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime in your device settings: Settings → your name → Subscriptions.

Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple — you can request one at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Account & data

You can delete your account and all associated data directly in the app: Settings → Account Details → Delete account. Your account and data are permanently deleted after 30 days; logging back in within that period cancels the deletion.

For data questions, see our Privacy Policy or email us.
