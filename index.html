Frequently Asked Questions

How do I record a lecture and turn it into notes?+

Tap the + button, choose “Record or upload audio”, and hit record. When the lecture ends, Notix transcribes the recording and generates a clean, structured summary automatically. You can also upload an existing audio file.

Can I turn PDF textbooks and slides into summaries?+

Yes. Upload any PDF or document and Notix produces a structured summary you can search, quiz yourself on, or turn into flashcards.

Can I make notes from YouTube videos?+

Yes. Paste a YouTube or web link and Notix turns the video or article into notes, quizzes, and flashcards.

Is Notix free?+

Notix is free to start — you can create notes, quizzes, and flashcards without paying. Notix Pro unlocks unlimited recording hours, unlimited quiz questions, and video & PDF summarization with monthly or annual plans. No commitment, cancel anytime.

Is it okay to use Notix at my school?+

Notix is a study aid: it helps you capture and review your own course materials. Recording policies differ between schools, so check your institution's rules on recording lectures before you hit record.

What languages does Notix support?+

The app is available in English, Spanish, French, German, and Russian — and your summaries and quizzes follow your chosen language.
